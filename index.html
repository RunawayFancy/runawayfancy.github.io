Biography

Jiheng Duan is a prospective PhD student of physics at the Department of Physics, University of Rochester. His research interests include semiconductor quantum dots, superconducting quantum computing, and quantum information processing. He worked on designing a quantum algorithm for searching collisions of SHA-1 in Prof. Hou Ian’s group at University of Macau. He focus on exploring new methods to fix the distortion of flux pulses for achieving high fidelity z-control on flux tunable superconducting qubits with Dr. Fei Yan at Beijing Academy of Quantum Information Science during 05/2023 - 08/2023. Now he join Prof. John Nichol’s group for proceeding charge noise in double dots spin qubits and nano-fabrication techniques.
