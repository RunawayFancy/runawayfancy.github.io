Welcome

Hi, this is Jiheng Duan, an undergraduate student major in physics from the University of Macau. You can call me Gigi or just Jiheng.

I am interested in quantum computation, quantum information processing, and atomic, molecular, and optical (AMO) physics, including the design of quantum algorithms, coherent measurement, and quantum annealing. Currently, I am focusing on designing a quantum algorithm for solving the Shortest vector problem to find a quantum solver of the learning with error problem. Also, I worked as an assistant with a Ph.D. student to measure a collective lamb shift of two qubits. For the ongoing study, I am reading books about quantum optics, field theory, advanced quantum mechanics, and AMO physics. More about my research experience can be found in the Research part.

You can find more about my current and previous research results on my homepage. Notes of several physics and mathematics courses collected personally will be posted on my site, also. As an undergraduate physics student, I also have a wide range of interests in programming and algorithms. The relevant files and projects are pushed on my GitHub. For other interests, I was a Minecraft Redstone player who ran a server. Several videos and research about the mechanism of the game were published on my YouTube channel. These are shown in the Miscellany part.
