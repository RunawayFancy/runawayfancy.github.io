My Redstone-tech Server

I launched a survival multiple player (SMP) server focusing on redstone technology under game version 1.15.2 and above. We got a huge success in the application of light-update suppression in mob farms, especially in setting up light-update suppressors and update suppressors for building such farms. We also have wonderful players who built awesome buildings and architecture. Moreover, several mini-game like ultra-hardcore (UHC) survival was held for entertainment.
